About me

Engineer by education, communicator by passion, and storyteller by purpose, I help people and technology understand each other better.

My career has taken me from engineering classrooms to boardrooms, from training AI systems to coaching human communication.

My work focuses on bridging the gap between artificial intelligence models, technical documentation, and front-end interface quality. I help teams ensure the integrity of training datasets while designing clear, accessible information frameworks.

I've led large annotation teams, facilitated executive conversations, mentored senior leaders in communication, and spoken on stages ranging from corporate workshops to international speech competitions. Today, I'm passionate about transforming complex ideas into clear, impactful experiences through technical communication and storytelling.

As a keynote speaker and International speech competitor, I've learned that whether you're documenting a product or addressing an audience, the goal remains the same: make the complex understandable and inspire people to take action.
